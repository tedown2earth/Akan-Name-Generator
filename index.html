What is an Akan Name? In West African countries moreso;Ghana, Ivory Coast and Togo, it is a culture where they frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born.

These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, or an ancestor's middle name.
